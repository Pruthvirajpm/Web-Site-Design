Inception Movie

Details Information
Title Inception
Director/Producer Christopher Nolan
Leading Actor Leonardo DiCaprio
Leading Actress Marion Cotillard
Rating PG-13
Description Inception is a science-fiction action film that explores the concept of entering and manipulating dreams. Leonardo DiCaprio plays a skilled thief who is hired to enter the subconscious of a CEO to plant an idea. As he and his team delve deeper into the man's mind, they face unexpected challenges and dangerous adversaries. The movie features stunning visual effects and a mind-bending plot that keeps the audience guessing until the very end.
